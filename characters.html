Truyan Saigas of Ganshun

Appearance/Identity-
Truyan Saigas is a teenage girl that was born to a fortune teller mother, and a Barladan Father. A Barladan is a foreign ethic group of traders within the context of the book. They are distinct for their blue hair, and are often seen as distrustful, or shady. Truyan saigas, beingg born to a Barladan father, has blue hair genetically, but she must hide it in public to avoid suspicion.
Background
When Truyan was only a child, her father went on a business trip overseas and never returned. He had completely disappeared from her life, and she had never seen him again. After their father left, the family began to fall apart. Truyan's mother gained a severe gambling addiction, and would gamble away all the money they had. Truyan had to make ends meet by painting and selling her artwork. Because she was Barladan, she had to hide her blue hair in public, and she would have to dress as a noble in order to blend in and sell her artwork to high ranking officials. As a child, Truyan loved her father very much and was obsessed with adventures. she wanted to travel the world overseas. she learned and loved to paint, because she could imagine all the adventures she could go on. In childhood, her father gave her a special paintbrush, and she would use that paintbrush to paint whatever she wanted. she would play games with her sisters, including a special imaginaroy game called Magic Paintbrush. because she grew up mostly poor, she is a thin, frail girl, with pale skin. Family-
Truyan Saigas has a very close relationship with her family. She has a mother, who always claims she could tell the future, and two sisters Nomi, and Falina.

Falina

Tru's sister Falina is exceptionally beautiful, but exceptionally vain as well. she enjoys her self by flirting with boys, and looking at her own reflection. she has always dreamed of the finer things in life.

Nomi

Nomi, on the other hand has always been focused on studies, and always willing to know more. she loves to read and had been a child prodigy. she had always outshined her sisters. Despite being the youngest sister, she had been able to read better than both her sisters by the age of 4. she enjoys books, sciences, and invention. Portrayed as: Intelligent, Brave

"Baba" Arban

Arban is the name of Truyan's father. Arban was a Barladan, an ethnic group that was often discriminated agains within the mortal realm. people often didnt trust him and he was usually looked at in fear.Despite this, Tru's mother still married him, and truly loves him even if she never showed it. He would work overseas a lot of the time, and would rarely ever get to see his family. He loved his three children very much.

Truyan's Mother

Truyan's mother is a fortune teller. she is a very superstitious person, and she truly believes that she can tell the future. After her husband disappeared overseas, she became a gambling addict, and would often gamble away all the money they had. As reckless as she had become, she loved her daughters with everything in her.
She is portrayed as: A very determined person who wants nothing but the best for her children, even if it is shallow.

Elang / Elang'anmi of Ai'long

Elanganmi is the grandson of the Dragon King, Nazayun of the underwater dragon realm of Ai'Long.
Appearance/Identity-
Elang'anmi is the lord of the Westerly Seas, prince of the Third Supreme Kingdom, and the illigitimate grandson of the dragon king, Nazayun. his father was a dragon and his mother was a human. While it is common for dragon princes to be wed to human mortals, the child either comes out as a dragon, or a human, but never a hybrid of the two. Elang was a hybrid, so his face is half human, with pale skin and yellow eyes, and the other half of his face is dragon-like, with blue scales, and a gray-ish blue eye. Elang'anmi's main goal is to overthrow his grandfather, Nazayun. However, because of his hybrid nature, he was seen as an abomination by hsi grandfatehr, and thereforme was banished to the human realm. the only way he is to regurn to Ai'Long is if he found his heavenly match, to be wed to.

Family-
Elang'anmi's most important family member is his grandfather, Nazayun. Nazayun is the Eternal Dragon King of the underwater realm of Ai'Long. He is a powerful and wise deity that has lived for hundreds of years ruling over the realm. Nazayun is a strict traditional figure, and he absolutely despises his grandson, Elang. Because Elang is a hybrid human Nazayun sees him as an abomination, and a disgrace to the family, and therefore banished Elang from the realm. In doing so, he had also taken Elang's Dragon pearl, which is the root of all his emotion and feeling, and is essentialy a dragon's heart.
