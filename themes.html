A Forgery of Fate--Themes

Love/Romance over Obligation

Another major theme in the book is love over obligation. it is the entire reason Truyan and Elang met, and fell in love. at the start of the book, Elang had convinced Truyan Saigas to be bonded to him in a faux matrimony, so Elgan is able to return to the Dragon Realm. In return, Tru will recieve 3 chests worth of riches that would support her family for the rest of their lives.
"A simple transaction, A fake marriage and in return, Tru's family is set for life. However, at the end of the book, Tru and Elang are married in genuine matrimony, rather than an obligatory one.

"It would be so easy to end things, to divorce myself of Elang and his war with the Dragon King, and mark this as the beginning of the rest of my life. The only problem? I was starting to realize I loved him back." (397)

Some of the books themes are even directly quoted in the text.

"The strongest bridge is made of truth," (Elang'anmi, pp. 209)

"Deception will crumble, given enough time." (Elang'anmi, pp. 209)
